Biography:

Clutch's life prior to his rescue from dog jail is mostly a mystery. He doesn't like to talk about it much, but, from what have learned, he led a ruff life. Now, Clutch has learned to enjoy the finer things in life...treats, walks to the park, belly rubs, and all the naps he can get. You won't find a cuter rat-terrier/chihuahua/german-shepard/pekingese/poodle (he's been tested) dog around!
